### Post 6: Educational Content - Debugging AI Systems

**Type:** How-To / Educational | **Engagement:** Medium-High

---

**Post Content:**

🔍 **How to Debug a Hallucinating LLM (5-Step Framework)**

Your LLM is hallucinating. You know it. Your users know it. But where do you start debugging?

Here's a framework we've refined with dozens of customers:

**Step 1: Identify the pattern**
Is it hallucinating on specific topics? Specific data formats? Specific prompts? Use observability data to find the pattern. Random hallucinations are harder to fix than systematic ones.

**Step 2: Isolate the cause**
Is it the model? The prompt? The retrieval? The context window? Trace the request end-to-end. Where does the quality degrade?

**Step 3: Test hypotheses**
Change one variable at a time. Better prompt? Different model? More context? Measure the impact.

**Step 4: Implement the fix**
Once you've identified the root cause, implement your fix. Deploy to a small percentage of traffic first.

**Step 5: Monitor and measure**
Use observability to confirm the fix worked. Measure the improvement. Document what you learned.

**The key insight:** You can't fix what you can't see. Observability isn't just for debugging—it's for understanding your AI systems deeply enough to improve them systematically.

This is what we built OpsNexAI for. Real-time visibility into every request, so you can debug faster and ship better.

## Part 5: Best Practices for LinkedIn Success

### Content Strategy

**Focus on Value Over Promotion:** LinkedIn audiences are skeptical of overly promotional content. Instead, share insights, frameworks, and lessons learned. For every promotional post, share 3-4 educational or thought leadership posts.

**Tell Stories:** People connect with stories, not statistics. Share customer success stories, team milestones, and lessons learned. Make your content relatable and human.

**Be Consistent:** Post 3-4 times per week on a consistent schedule. LinkedIn's algorithm rewards consistency. Pick times when your audience is most active (typically 8-10am or 5-6pm on weekdays).

**Optimize for Engagement:** Use questions to encourage comments. Share controversial (but thoughtful) takes to spark discussion. Respond to every comment within the first hour to boost visibility.

### Hashtag Strategy

Use 5-8 relevant hashtags per post. Mix popular hashtags (#AI, #Technology) with niche hashtags (#AIObservability, #LLMOps). Place hashtags at the end of your post for cleaner formatting.

**Recommended hashtags for OpsNexAI:**

### Employee Advocacy

Encourage your team to share company posts and content. Employee posts typically get 8x more engagement than company posts. Provide easy-to-share content and make it simple for your team to amplify your message.

### Community Building

Don't just broadcast—participate. Comment on posts from industry leaders, potential customers, and competitors. Join relevant LinkedIn groups and contribute thoughtfully. Build relationships before asking for business.

### Metrics to Track

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
- Follower growth rate (target: 10-15% monthly growth)
- Post engagement rate (target: 3-5% engagement per post)
- Click-through rate (target: 1-2% for posts with links)
- Conversion rate (track signups from LinkedIn)
